Introduction

Arms Race is a modded Minecraft game mode where two teams of players race to build the best weapons, fight their way to the enemy base, and destroy it.

This page presents all the information for both new and experienced players.

Basic Rules

  • Players are divided into Red and Blue teams at the start of each game.
  • During the grace period, the teams build tools & weapons on their home islands.
  • Once the time is up, players may leave their home islands to attack the enemy team.
  • Whichever team destroys the other team's three beacons wins the game!

Additional Rules

  • Do not place a Mekanism teleporter in enemy territory.
  • Air vehicle dismounting is only allowed up to the enemy mountain.
  • Do not break blocks that are not exposed to the sky in enemy territory.
  • Tunneling is not allowed.
  • F3+B is banned; F3+A is still allowed.
  • Banned items include beds, nether portals, jetpacks, gliders, and parachutes.
  • Riot shields, explosive Barrett ammo, and Javelin top-attack mode are banned as too powerful.
